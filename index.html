Truckfights

A game by Nathan Watts, Colin Watts, Quinn Collins, and Brian Rappaport

What is Truckfights?

Truckfights is a mobile and PC game that combines the speed and style of Mad Max with the strategy of Lovers in a Dangerous Spacetime. Truckfights is also cooperative online multiplayer, so you can play with your friends no matter how far away. Pilot two 18-wheelers welded together and geared up for war across the desert, avoid obstacles and shoot down enemy pursuit vehicles.

How do I play Truckfights?

The controls for Truckfights are simple: use the joystick to move, the green button to get into and use turrets, and the red button to get out of turrets. On PC, WASD keys replace the joystick, the E key replaces the green button, and the R key replaces the red button. You can also view the controls in-game.

Development

Truckfights was developed using the Unity Game Engine by Nathan Watts, Colin Watts, Quinn Collins, and Brian Rappaport. Truck and car art by Nathan Watts, backgrounds by Brian Rappaport.
